# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior includes:**
- Being respectful and considerate
- Welcoming newcomers
- Accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what's best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**
- Harassment or discrimination
- Trolling or insulting comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and when representing the project in public spaces.
